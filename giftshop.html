Gift shop service

Open Daily 10am – 5pm (last entry 4pm)

Explore the Blue Bay Gift Shop for the perfect souvenir of your visit to Blue Planet Aquarium!

The Blue Bay Gift Shop is the home to some of the best sea life products to treat yourself or a loved one. From stationary and cuddly toys, to jewellery and magnets there is something for everyone!

We work with suppliers to reduce the single use plastic used for shop items, with some of our cuddly toy ranges being made into the softest toys out of recycled plastic. We also work with them to reduce single use plastic packaging with steps being made in the right direction with every order.

Check out our selection of accessories, with genuine shark tooth necklaces and the cutest clownfish keyring, plus you’ll never be late again with our fishy themed, colourful slap watches.

We have lots of Aquarium branded items so you’ll never forget your visit to Blue Planet Aquarium, or visit again and again to browse our ever changing range of aquatic goodies available!
