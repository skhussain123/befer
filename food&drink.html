Food and Drinking shop

Nautilus Kitchen



You have heard of sleeping with the fishes, now how about eating with them?

Submerge under the water for a unique dining experience – no wetsuit required!

Deep sea dining in our Nautilus Kitchen offers an array of freshly prepared hot and cold food, snacks and drinks to suit every hungry tummy!

Why not stop by during your visit?


Ice Cream Parlour



Fancy a treat? We’ve got lots of delicious flavours of ice cream to choose from in our beautifully themed Ice Cream Parlour!

Choose your favourite flavour (or two) and enjoy our outdoor area with ample picnic benches, a playground for the children and watch our impressive Dalmatian Pelicans. A fantastic way to unwind!
