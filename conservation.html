Conservation

There is a growing need to preserve our World’s waters and the animals that call it home. Pressures on our environment continue to grow, together we can help reduce these pressures and conserve wildlife for the future…

Our team are passionate about conservation and work tirelessly to ensure our magnificent animals are protected for the benefit of future generations.

Blue Planet Aquarium has many endangered species whom we showcase to raise awareness of why they have become so critical to our ocean environment, from iconic Sand Tiger Sharks to freshwater tequila split fins.


Charities we work with

Bumblebee Conservation Trust

We have joined Bumblebee Conservation Trust with our Nature Walk. Aiming to support local eco systems and share tips to protect local inhabitants.

Bite back

Working hand in hand with Bite-Back, leading UK charity dedicated to shark and marine conservation. Bite-Back’s goal is to combat the overfishing and overconsumption of wild marine life. .


Beach Cleans


At Blue Planet Aquarium we want to help clean up our local beaches to create a better environment for all.

We are always keen to enlist the help of the wider community to join us on our beach cleans. Email us at info@blueplanetaquarium.co.uk to find out when our next beach clean will be!
